Prof. Benedict Jun MA
---

Assistant Professor, INTR

As the leader of this lab, Benedict Jun Ma is a tenure-track Assistant Professor in the Intelligent Transportation Thrust at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), where he is leading the Intelligent Logistics and Supply Chain Analytics (iLSCA) Lab. Prior to this, he was a Postdoctoral Associate at Massachusetts Institute of Technology, affiliated with the MIT Center for Transportation and Logistics (CTL) and the MIT Digital Supply Chain Lab. He received his PhD from the Department of Data and Systems Engineering at The University of Hong Kong. He was also a visiting student at Mines Paris – PSL University, Erasmus University Rotterdam, HKUST, and NHH.

You can find more at his [faculty page](https://facultyprofiles.hkust-gz.edu.cn/faculty-personal-page/MA-BenedictJun/junm) or the [Publications page](https://benedictjunma.github.io/publications/).
