Prof. Benedict Jun MA
---

Assistant Professor, INTR

As the leader of the iLSCA lab, Benedict is a tenure-track Assistant Professor in INTR at HKUST(GZ). Prior to this, he was a Postdoctoral Associate at Massachusetts Institute of Technology, affiliated with the MIT Center for Transportation and Logistics (CTL) and the MIT Digital Supply Chain Lab. He received his PhD from the Department of Data and Systems Engineering at The University of Hong Kong. He was also a visiting researcher at Mines Paris – PSL University, Erasmus University Rotterdam, HKUST, and NHH.

You can find more at his [faculty page](https://facultyprofiles.hkust-gz.edu.cn/faculty-personal-page/MA-BenedictJun/junm) or the [Publications page](https://benedictjunma.github.io/publications/).
