Welcome to Intelligent Logistics & Supply Chain Analytics (iLSCA) Lab!
---

Based at the [Thrust of Intelligent Transportation (INTR), Systems Hub, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou)](https://www.hkust-gz.edu.cn/academics/hubs-and-thrust-areas/systems-hub/intelligent-transportation/), our dynamic team currently consists of 11 motivated researchers, including 2 full-time Ph.D. students and 9 Research Assistants. 

Together, we are dedicated to solving the critical challenges facing modern logistics and supply chains by deeply integrating Intelligent Transportation, Smart Warehousing, Autonomous Drone & Robotics Systems, and Large Language Models (LLMs).
