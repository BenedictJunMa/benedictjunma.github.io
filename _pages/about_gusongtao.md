Mr. Songtao GU

Research Assistant

Songtao Gu joined the research team in January 2026. He will receive his Bachelor of Engineering degree from the joint program of Southwest Jiaotong University and the University of Leeds in June 2026. Following this, he will proceed to Tongji University to pursue his Master's degree in September 2026. His research interests lie in the field of Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS), with a specific focus on Connected and Automated Vehicle (CAV) platoon coordination and mixed traffic flow optimization.
