Ms. Xincan WANG
---

Ph.D. Student in INTR

Xincan Wang will join the lab as a Ph.D. student in September 2026. She received her Master's degree from South China University of Technology in 2026 and her Bachelor's degree from Guangxi University, both in Computer Science and Technology. Her research interests include combinatorial optimization, evolutionary computation, path planning, and human-robot collaboration.

Representative publication: 

<u>Wang, X.-C.</u>, Gong, Y.-J., Huang, T., Xu, H., & Zhang, J. (2025). Long-Distance Charge-Route Planning for Electric Vehicles: A Multi-Solution Approach. *IEEE Transactions on Transportation Electrification*. [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1109/TTE.2025.3563094)
